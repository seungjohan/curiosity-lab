# Design Spec: Cooking Vocabulary Expansion (Steaks)

## 1. Purpose
Expand the `wiki/cooking/vocabulary.md` file to include premium meat terms from Spain and Italy, bridging regional specialties with general culinary knowledge.

## 2. Content Changes

### 🇪🇸 Spanish Section
Update the Spanish vocabulary table to include:
- **Txuleta:** A thick, bone-in ribeye steak or chop, traditionally grilled. While Basque in origin, it is a hallmark of high-quality beef across Spain.
  - *Example:* "Savoring a perfectly grilled txuleta at a traditional Spanish asador."

### 🇮🇹 Italian Section
Update the Italian vocabulary table to include:
- **Bistecca:** The general Italian term for steak.
  - *Example:* "Requesting the daily bistecca selection."
- **Bistecca alla Fiorentina:** A thick-cut T-bone or porterhouse steak, traditionally grilled rare over wood or charcoal.
  - *Example:* "Bistecca alla Fiorentina is best shared among friends in Florence."

## 3. Implementation Plan
1.  **Edit** `wiki/cooking/vocabulary.md`:

    - Replace the placeholder in the Italian table with **Bistecca** and **Bistecca alla Fiorentina**.
2.  **Update** `wiki/log.md`: Record the addition of Spanish and Italian steak terms.
