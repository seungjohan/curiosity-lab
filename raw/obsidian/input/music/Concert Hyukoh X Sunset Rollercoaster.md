## Thoughts
- I’ve been wishing the moment for a long time. so, at the first time, when they started to play a song It made to thrill, and I was almost gonna cry. Excepting the indie band, or public college festival, It was my first time to buy a concert ticket and experience the music culture, and I completely understood why people love music, and eager to attend the concert in person. It made me want to be a musician. It was amazing 3 hours.
- Waiting in line before the concert drained me, but definitely worth it, and It was also fun and interesting experience. We had to wait in line by our booking number even though It was a standing seat. When we entered the standing seat, we were covered by full of people waiting in the front, standing. We did a stand for almost an entire day, so our legs were bloated a bit and tight. But after the time went by, we could go forward a little bit more, and it was pretty satisfying.
- I thought 'wasn't it too expensive for the one concert?', but definitely worth it, if I love the singers, and if I want to feel that atmosphere with the crowd, and the vibrating air with the sound. Then, we should go!
	- Besides, also we I felt was 'Band Hyukoh' is now really major band but still I think their genre belongs to the minority, not commercialise or trendy. But when I was at that concert, all the people went there to love and enjoy that music together. I don't need to worry about what other people's tastes. All I need is just to focus on my way.
- **I want to keep investing my time, and money for experiencing this type of music culture. I want to keep exposing myself in this. It was thrilled, and amazing.**

	- A scene with two guitarists consistently changed
	- Close shots of each player.
- Hyuk's voice and soul were almost the same as the ones on the album. I barely find the difference between those two.
