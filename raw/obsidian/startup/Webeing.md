### About

‘Webeing’ is the B2B2C food e-commerce platform service to provide leftover foods that are close to an expiration date or stock items at a lower price, especially fruits and small dishes. We encourage customers to join our environmental activities.

We wanted to solve the problem of food waste and drinking culture in Korean restaurants.

The owner of small business can sell leftover food near the expired date and customers can buy the food what they want with cheap price.

We were an ESG startup for leading of protecting Earth by providing other services like environments account, goods, and crowd-funding.

### Problem

When I lived near my university, I used to cross a main street full of trash. But we could often observe the street in the downtown area covered with trash, cigarette butt, and food waste. 

Due to Korea’s 회식 and drinking culture, significant food waste occurs, especially in downtown, and university areas. 
Restaurants discard excess food that’s still edible, leading to both financial losses and environmental impact.

The biggest problems are that we are so used to leaving a lot of food and throwing those away on the street. They don’t care about the environmental problems and the cost of removal.

- Food waste
- The number of single-person households is increasing
- The trend of consumption is changing after COVID-19 

- Cultural norms around ordering excess food during group dining (회식) lead to substantial waste, particularly in urban restaurants.

### Market & User Research

- Conducted qualitative and quantitative research with over 300 local citizens in 위례신도시, 성남 (Wirye New Town, Seongnam Cit**y**)
- Validated customer interest in sustainable food purchasing.
- Identified business pain points in managing unsold or near-expiry inventory.

### Problem Solving

We provide stock items or foods closed with an expired date for cheap price so that owners can sell the stock as much as possible and customers can buy food or items nearby their houses.

We also make the services environmental shop, environmental activities and financial part.

Users can save the earth by their consumption and they can get services like getting discounts or buying environmental goods such as echo bags in the shop.

We can encourage consumers to get interested in the environment and join environmental activities together. For example, a Campaign for cleaning the trash or One-day class of making items with trash.

This can be the perfect contactless way to buy food

### Result

We had two teams. Business and Product. I belonged in both departments. There were 3 members in the Business department and 5 members in the Software Product department.

Our service targeted the specific area Wirye, Seongnam-si, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea (위례신도시).

Research (quantitative & qualitative). We interviewed over 300 interviewers to validate our hypothesis, did pivot several times and changed 4 times for the prototype.

We released one beta website service and after that, we released a new Native Web-App SaaS service.

We made a small revenue.
