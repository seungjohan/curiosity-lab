- 오랑대 cliff and sunrise pavilion -  not as obvious as 해동용궁사
- 기장시장
- Someone harvesting seaweed or preparing seafood
- The beach reflecting late afternoon light for nostalgic, almost cinematic feel
- 아홉산숲

Tips
- Use reflections like puddles, sunglasses, glass to capture landmarks in an unusual way
- Frame a location through a natural frame, like tree branches, a window, or an arch
- Capture seasonal contrast like tourists holding umbrellas during the monsoon
- Night is good - like 해동용궁사 at night, lit against the waves
