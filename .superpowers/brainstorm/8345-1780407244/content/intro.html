Decision 1: Blog & Portfolio Structure

How should the primary structure look? Your reference, MJ Kang, uses separate domains for portfolio and blog. Others combine them.

A

Separated (Two Sites)

e.g., portfolio.me and blog.me. Keeps personal branding and deep-dive content distinct.

B

Combined (Unified Site)

Everything under one roof. Better for SEO and "all-in-one" personal identity.

C

Hybrid (Sub-path/Sub-domain)

e.g., site.com/blog or blog.site.com. Unified branding with structural separation.
