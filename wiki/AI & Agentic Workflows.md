# AI & Agentic Workflows

Investigation into Large Language Models (LLMs), AI Agents, and the Model Context Protocol (MCP).

## Core Concepts
- **LLMs as Operating Systems**: Moving beyond simple chat to executing complex tasks.
- **Agentic Workflows**: Multi-step processes where AI agents collaborate or iterate.
- **MCP (Model Context Protocol)**: Standardizing how models access tools and data.
