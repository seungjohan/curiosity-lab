# Social Interactions

## The Philosophy of Small Talk
- **Energy Generation**: Small talk is a tool to create positive daily energy, even if it feels "inefficient" or "un-Korean" at times.
- **Cultural Diplomacy**: Small acts of kindness to foreigners can represent the country positively and change their perspective of Korea.
- **Human Connection**: Brief interactions with neighbors or fellow runners (morning runs) make the world feel brighter.

## Judging Others
- **The Mirror Effect**: When we judge someone else's life or "level," we are often reflecting our own insecurities.
- **Inherent Value**: Everyone has their own "spark" and way of living. Judging them by my frame is a mistake.
