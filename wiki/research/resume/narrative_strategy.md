# Proposed Resume Narrative Approaches

Based on your background at **Upstage**, **Bear Robotics**, and your **Composer** identity, I propose three distinct directions for your resume redesign.

## 🏢 Option 1: The 0→1 System Architect
**Focus:** Building foundations where none exist.
- **Narrative Wedge:** "I thrive in the chaos of early-stage AI, building the product frameworks and operational systems that turn research into revenue."
- **Key Highlight:** Your work setting up the initial product structures at Upstage or Bear Robotics.
- **Ideal For:** Engineering-heavy startups (FuriosaAI, Rebellions) and early-stage ventures.
- **Tone:** Technical, robust, operational.

## 🎼 Option 2: The Sensory Synthesizer (Composer-PM)
**Focus:** The intersection of Multimodal AI and Creative Intuition.
- **Narrative Wedge:** "Building AI for humans requires an ear for harmony. I leverage my background as a composer to synthesize complex sensory data into intuitive product experiences."
- **Key Highlight:** Connecting the "patterns" of music to the "patterns" of Video Understanding (Twelve Labs) or Voice Synthesis.
- **Ideal For:** Creative Tech leaders (Twelve Labs, Synthesia, ElevenLabs, Scatter Lab).
- **Tone:** Visionary, intuitive, differentiated.

## 🌍 Option 3: The Global Scaling Bridge
**Focus:** Cross-border product leadership.
- **Narrative Wedge:** "I bridge the gap between localized technical excellence and global market scale, having led product efforts in both Dublin and Seoul."
- **Key Highlight:** Your ability to navigate the cultural and business nuances of the Ireland/Korea axis.
- **Ideal For:** Growth-stage unicorns with global footprints (Sendbird, Moloco, Wrtn).
- **Tone:** Strategic, business-oriented, communicative.
