> [!IMPORTANT] Key Takeaway
> **Why this matters:** Spanish cuisine (especially Tapas/Pintxos) represents "modular" and iterative testing of features in a social context.
> **How to use it:** Study the balance between traditional rustic flavors and avant-garde techniques (molecular gastronomy).
> **Informs:** Innovation within constraints and modularity.

# 🇪🇸 Cocina Española

## 🍲 Platos Emblemáticos
- **Paella**: The ultimate "platform" dish from Valencia, demonstrating how a base (rice) can host diverse and modular components.
- **Fideuà**: A seafood dish similar to paella but using noodles, representing a creative "fork" of a traditional architecture.
