# 🍴 Selected Restaurant Sources

This page documents the primary culinary communities and guides referred to for identifying exceptional restaurants globally. The data is periodically updated from these trusted sources.

## 🌟 Primary Communities & Guides

### 1. Michelin Guide
The gold standard for fine dining globally. It focuses on the quality of ingredients, mastery of technique, the personality of the chef in their cuisine, value for money, and consistency.
*   **Focus:** Technical perfection, service, and consistency.
*   **Scoped Data:** 3-Star and 2-Star establishments globally.

### 2. The World's 50 Best Restaurants
A global ranking based on the opinions of over 1,000 international culinary experts. It often highlights more avant-garde and culturally significant restaurants that might not fit the traditional Michelin mold.
*   **Focus:** Innovation, cultural impact, and global trends.
*   **Scoped Data:** Top 100 list.

### 3. Guía Repsol (Spain & Portugal)
The premier culinary guide for the Iberian Peninsula. It provides a more localized and deep-dive perspective on Spanish and Portuguese gastronomy, from traditional "casas de comidas" to avant-garde temples.
*   **Focus:** Iberian excellence, product focus, and regional authenticity.
*   **Scoped Data:** 3 Soles, 2 Soles, and 1 Sol awardees.

### 4. World's 101 Best Steak Restaurants
A specialized guide focused exclusively on the art of meat and grilling. It evaluates the breed, aging, cut, and fire-management of the world's best steakhouses.
*   **Focus:** Product-driven excellence, fire management, and meat aging.
*   **Scoped Data:** Full 101 list.
