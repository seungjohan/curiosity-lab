# 🇹🇭 Thailand: The Balance of 5 Flavors

Thai cuisine is celebrated for its vibrant colors, aromatic herbs, and the deliberate balance of five fundamental flavors: sour, sweet, salty, bitter, and spicy.

## 🥘 Signature Dishes
- **Chim Chum (จิ้มจุ่ม):** A popular Thai street food hot pot made with a clear herbal broth (lemongrass, galangal, kaffir lime leaves), traditionally cooked in a clay pot over charcoal.
- **Tom Yum Goong:** A spicy and sour soup with shrimp.
- **Pad Thai:** Stir-fried rice noodles with a balance of sweet, sour, and salty flavors.
- **Som Tum:** Green papaya salad.
