# 🧅 Soupe à l’oignon (French Onion Soup)

A classic French soup based on meat stock and onions, typically served gratinéed with croutons and cheese on top.

## 🥣 Key Features
- **Caramelization**: Slow-cooking onions to extract sweetness.
- **Deglazing**: Using wine or stock to pull up the browned bits (fond) from the pan.
- **Gratinée**: The crust of melted Gruyère or Comté cheese.
