# 🥖 Baguette

The iconic French long bread, defined by its crisp crust and holes in the crumb.

## 🥣 Key Features
- **Autolyse**: Resting the flour and water to improve gluten structure.
- **Grignage**: The scoring of the bread before baking to control the expansion.
- **Vapeur**: Adding steam to the oven to create the characteristic shine and crispness of the crust.
