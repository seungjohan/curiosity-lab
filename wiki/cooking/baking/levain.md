# 🍞 Le vain (Sourdough Starter)

A natural leavening agent made of flour and water, fermented by wild yeast and lactic acid bacteria.

## 🥣 Key Features
- **Feeding Ratio**: The proportion of starter, flour, and water.
- **Lactic Acid Bacteria**: Responsible for the characteristic sour flavor.
- **Peak Maturity**: The point at which the starter is most active and ready for baking.
