# Dreams & Hobbies

## Music & Composing
- **Childhood Dreams**: Rediscovering the spark of creativity that was suppressed by "reality" and career focus.
- **Trigger**: A microphone gift from Muireann started a journey of cover recordings and self-study.
- **Study Areas**: Harmony, Composition, MIDI, Orchestration.
- **Vision**: To compose original music, collaborate with artists, and eventually perform. Even "impossible" goals like the Berlin Philharmonic are worth the passion.

## Why Pursue "Unrealistic" Goals?
- It makes life colorful and gives a sense of agency that "normal" work might lack.
- The goal is not just the result, but the state of "believing in one's dream."
