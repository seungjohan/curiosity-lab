# Relationship Lessons

## Heartbreak & Moving On
- **Acceptance**: The hardest part of a breakup is accepting it's truly over, especially when the other person moves on quickly.
- **Self-Blame vs. Reality**: While there are things to regret (not being kind enough, being preoccupied), some factors (distance, career goals) make the split inevitable.
- **Psychological Toll**: Breakups can trigger physical and mental symptoms like tremors, insomnia, and anxiety. Recovery is a slow process of "still breaking up."

## Interpersonal Dynamics
- **Selective Generosity**: Be mindful of people who only take (information, networks, help) without giving back. 
- **The "Liking" Principle**: To be liked, start by looking for the good in people rather than their flaws.
- **Protecting Privacy**: Be cautious about sharing vulnerabilities (anxiety, etc.) with those who might use them against you or share them inappropriately.

## Significant Others
- **Mta / Ex**: Lessons on long-distance, career sacrifices, and the pain of a "harsh" goodbye.
- **Muireann**: A source of growth and creative trigger (the microphone gift). A partner who helps one "shine" is invaluable.
